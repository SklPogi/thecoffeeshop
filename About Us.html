This website was created to provide the best coffee on the go whether at home or at work, the coffee break gives alertness and improvement of your performance. Established on 1989, five coffee lover dreamt of having coffee shop and the coffee break was created.As years passes by the coffee shop was franchised in different places here in the Philippines. There are Eight branches in Luzon, Five branches in Visayas and Mindanao have Two. Coffee Break Shop is a perfect place to relax and having conversation with your love ones. While having our coffee, you can also review with your classmates, friends and so on. discovered out of devastated heart and rebuild with a bolder product of love. Our coffee shop os inspired by the story of our founder, Maria Clara. Just like any other coffee, bitterness is always present. It will always depend to you if you want to add a great amount of sugar to make it sweet or deal with the taste. After losing his one great love and everything starts to fall apart, Maria Clara kept on fighting for the love that never was. Thats the time she deciced to cope with the bitter taste. As time goes by, she tried to pour some sugar and realize that it is better than just dealing with it. She let go of her one and only love and was able to meet her true love. That's the time she found her way back to herself. She discovered her great passion for coffee shop. The name " Coffee Break" comes from its genesis. The siogan "Richness worth a second cup" is an encouragement for the people not to be afraid of loving again and to give chances for growth and discovery.
